## What this project is
We analyzed COMP110 survey data to see whether students who want more supports features, such as more pre-lecture videos, also reported higher difficult with content understanding

## Key results
- As interest in more pre-lecture videos increases, average difficulty increases and average understanding decreases.
- Students who most strongly want a livestream option report higher difficulty and lower understanding.
- The highest response levels had large group sizes, so the trends at the high end reflect many responses.
